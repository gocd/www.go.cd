GoCD's ELASTIC AGENTS
GIVES YOU THE FLEXIBILITY AND SAVINGS YOU NEED

Optimize Utilization and Costs Savings with Elastic Agents

GoCD operates on a server-agent model where a server communicates with many agents. Agents are the workers in GoCD ecosystem. Rather than having dedicated idle agents at all times, elastic agents allow you to scale up, with on-demand agents, based on the need of the task being executed. This saves you both time and costs.

Optimize Utilization & Save Costs

Use the Elastic Agent capability to spawn and terminate Agents to tailor to your individual projects. Scale flexibly to optimize your utilization and reduce costs.
